Marcus Mosiah Garvey Jr.

The Black Moses

A Timeline of the Life of Marcus Garvey:
  • 1887: Born in St. Ann's Bay, Jamaica
  • 1900: Begins apprenticeship at his godfather's printing business
  • 1906: Leaves St. Ann's Bay for Kingston, Jamaica
  • 1912: Moves to London to attend Birkbeck College
  • 1914: Along with Amy Ashwood, establishes the Universal Negro Improvement Association and African Communities League
  • 1917: Sets up the Black Star Line shipping company
  • 1923: Was sentenced to 5 years in prison on charges of "mail fraud"
  • 1928: After his prison release, establishes the People's Political Party in Jamaica
  • 1935: Passes away at the age of 52 in London, UK

    • For information on the life of Marcus Garvey, you can check out his Wikipedia entry.
